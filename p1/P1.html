Our Client

We have chosen to have an imaginary client for our project.

Client Name: Snow4days Inc.
Contact person: Mike Hawke

The client is a firm specializing in winter experiences around the world. They sell to travellers from several countries. They have no website, and want to have an online presence to reach more customers.

Purpose and goals

The goal of the site is to get potential customers interested in buying an experience-pack from the snow4days company. To reach out to more potential customers, they want to have an online presence and be reachable through search engines, like Google or Yahoo. The website should make information accessible to visitors, and make them want to purchase travel experiences.

For this reason the information should be presented in a easily digestible manner: easy to read sentences and pleasing graphical display. Fonts and colors should be chosen for readability, and navigation should be simple. The website should direct the visitor to purchasing possibilities: visiting address, email, and telephone.

Audience

Snow4Days delivers the total mountain experience. They are after skiers and snowboard riders with experience and preference for quality, and are willing to pay for it. They are passionate snow lovers, and they know what they want.

The company is not after amateurs or those only there for the party. Therefore, the website should focus on the skiing experience. The company wants to show potential customers “extreme slopes”, and “unique lines”.

Content of the website and organization

Main Page

The main page should invite the user to the page, and appetise them with a video and elegant design.

About us

Information about the company. How long they have been doing this, examples of earlier work. This website establishes credibility for the company, and makes the company appeal to our chosen audience.

Contact Us

Email, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter to quickly contact the company. Assure users that all forms of contact will be answered within 24 hours. How to order: email or telephone. This page gives the visitor the information they need to ask questions and to place an order for a winter experience.

Locations

Each of the location-pages includes a photograph of the location and two paragraphs of text with information relevant to the skiing customer. The text and information in these pages must appeal to our chosen audience, the experienced ski-lovers.

Subpages includes basic information like location with map, why go there, resort height, slope height, lifts, pists and price.

Sitemap

The website has a sitemap. This makes navigation simple and gives an overview of the website.

Preferences and Demands from Client

Necessary:

  • Sitemap
  • Site should have 99.9% uptime.
  • Clean and modern look
  • Pretty pictures
  • Easy to navigate
  • Be “totally rad” (client’s words.)

Desirable:

  • Responsive scaling
  • Cool logo

Technical Difficulties

We expect there might be potential challenge with version control system, if we choose to use one, but this is still preferable to the difficulty of not using one. There could also be potential performance issues relating to video.
