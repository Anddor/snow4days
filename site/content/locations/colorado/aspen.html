Four incredible mountains on one lift ticket. Two amazing towns with endless possibilities for when the slopes close. A winter vacation at Aspen Snowmass will engage your mind, body and spirit in ways no other winter vacation will.

Snowmass offers an abundance of deep snow and a vast expanse of terrain that includes 94 trails and the largest vertical descent of any resort in North America. Aspen Highlands may just be one ridge over from Aspen Mountain and Buttermilk, but it is truly in a world all its own. Adored by locals and elite skiers and riders, the mountain has a duct-tape-to-diamonds vibe.
